SHADES OF SURVIVAL

On the darkest shades of life I probably wished to inculcate my prettiest memory ever.... My fellow beings were so loyal to me. The day everyone prayed for me and the day they oppressed my roots... Everything are sorrowfully memorisable. I wished my past could take me back to the safest place of the world- mother's womb where I could here nothing, no castes, no creeds, no human build witchy superstitions, no evil eyes. Always people dare to peep into the life of others. Why are they not willing to give a solution for it rather than making the scenario hard? I was passing through the hardest times of my career. The sharpened words, cold hearted eyes, zero leisure work schedules.. everything made me dull as hell. I am from a middle class Orthodox family where self- settlement for women were too hard to accomplish. However, my parents took the action to act dumb before the infinte eyed villagers and boosted me to secure a good position. I found software developing as a good field to work on. I went for it. Months after I understood, the efforts I put in didn't work as expected. I started to survive hard under my boss. City life is so expensive! The peeping evil eyes made my street tavel during nights harder.
Finally I quit!
